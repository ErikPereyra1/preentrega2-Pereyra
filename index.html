Who are we? Whats our Mission?

We are a non lucrative organization based in Manhattan, New York. Our mission is to help any dogs in need of a home. Throughout the United States we provide homes for over 250 dogs in our temporary shelters. And over 4000 dogs have found a home thanks to HLD SHELTERS.

Where are our shelters located?

We first started in Manhattan, now we have shelters in Los Angeles and Miami. Across America there are 12 shelters in total. In 2021 we opened up in the UK as well! 2 bases located in the heart of the capital, London.

Shelters based in the US

We have 3 main bases in the US, 2 on the east coast and 1 on the west coast
